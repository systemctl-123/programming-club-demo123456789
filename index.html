// About

Who We Are

Founded in 2018 at the Dept. of Computer Science & Engineering, University of Dhaka.

01 Mission

To cultivate a culture of programming excellence — from competitive coding to building products that matter. We believe the best engineers practice deliberately and ship relentlessly.

02 What We Do

Weekly workshops, monthly contests, annual hackathons, ICPC preparation, open-source projects, and industry speaker sessions. Something for every level of programmer.

03 Our Community

142 active members across all years of the CSE program. We welcome students from all departments who love programming. You don't need to be a CS major — just bring curiosity and consistency. Our alumni network spans Google, Meta, local startups, and top grad programs worldwide.
